Tony Chat Data Safety & Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1. Overview

This document describes what data Tony Chat collects, how it's used, whether it's shared, and your control over it. This information supports both the Google Play Data Safety section and Apple App Privacy labels.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

Tony Chat shares data with the following third-party services strictly for functionality:

Google Gemini (AI Processing)

  • Data sent: Text input (AI Writer, Translator) and images (AI image tools)
  • Purpose: AI processing and content generation
  • Retention: Processed in real-time; not stored by Tony Chat after completion
  • Policy: Google Privacy Policy

fal.ai (Image Processing)

  • Data sent: Images for background removal, upscaling, and text removal
  • Purpose: AI image processing
  • Retention: Processed in real-time; not stored by Tony Chat after completion
  • Policy: fal.ai Privacy Policy

Telegram (Messaging Protocol)

  • Data sent: Account credentials, chat messages, contacts
  • Purpose: User authentication and secure messaging
  • Retention: Managed by Telegram's infrastructure
  • Policy: Telegram Privacy Policy

Supabase (Server Proxy)

  • Data sent: AI tool requests routed through secure proxy
  • Purpose: API proxy and news feed content delivery
  • Retention: Request logs retained for operational purposes only
  • Policy: Supabase Privacy Policy

4. Data Security

  • All AI requests are routed through our secure server proxy — no API keys are stored on your device
  • Chat messages use the Telegram protocol with end-to-end encryption support
  • Data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • AI tool processing is stateless — no input/output history is maintained on our servers

5. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
AI tool inputs/outputs Not retained (real-time processing only)
Chat messages Managed by Telegram (not stored by Tony Chat)
News articles cache Device memory only; cleared on app close
Device/crash info Up to 90 days for debugging purposes
Server request logs Up to 30 days for operational monitoring

6. Cookies & Tracking

  • Tony Chat is a native mobile application and does not use browser cookies.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics SDKs that track you across apps.
  • We do not participate in cross-app advertising or data broker networks.
  • The news feed in the Explore tab does not track which articles you read.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • Portability: Chat data is managed by Telegram and can be exported via Telegram's tools.
  • Opt-out: You can use AI tools without logging in (guest/browse mode). You can disconnect your Telegram account at any time.
  • Withdraw consent: Uninstalling the App withdraws consent for data collection.

8. Children's Data

Tony Chat is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Regional Compliance

  • Korea (PIPA): We comply with the Korean Personal Information Protection Act. Data processing is limited to stated purposes.
  • EU (GDPR): If applicable, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and data portability.
  • California (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. California residents may exercise their rights by contacting us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Safety & Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
