D. App Review Notes

Google Play — Review Notes

Tony Chat is an AI-powered assistant app that offers 6 unique AI tools (writing, translation, background removal, upscaling, text removal, image generation) and an AI-curated news feed. HOW TO TEST WITHOUT LOGIN: 1. Install and open the app 2. You will see the AI Hub with 6 tool cards — browse freely 3. Tap "Explore" tab to see the AI news feed 4. Tap "Settings" tab to see app preferences 5. All three tabs work fully without any account HOW TO TEST WITH LOGIN: 1. Tap any AI tool card (e.g., "AI Writer") 2. You will be prompted to log in via Telegram (phone number + SMS code) 3. After login, all AI tools are fully functional 4. "Chats" tab shows your Telegram conversations DIFFERENTIATION FROM TELEGRAM: - Tony Chat is an AI-first application, not a messaging client - 6 original AI tools not available in Telegram - AI-curated news feed not available in Telegram - App opens to AI Hub by default, not chat list - Custom branding, icon, and yellow theme - Uses Telegram's open messaging protocol (similar to how email clients use IMAP/SMTP)

App Store — Review Notes

Tony Chat is an AI-powered assistant app with 6 unique AI tools and an AI-curated news feed. Messaging is a secondary feature powered by Telegram's open protocol. TESTING WITHOUT AN ACCOUNT (recommended first): 1. Launch the app — lands on AI Hub (4 tabs visible) 2. Browse all 6 AI tool cards freely (Tony AI tab) 3. Tap "Explore" to see the AI news feed with category filters 4. Tap "Settings" to see app preferences → No login required for browsing TESTING WITH AN ACCOUNT: 1. Tap any AI tool (e.g., "AI Writer") → login prompt appears 2. Enter any phone number with active Telegram account 3. Enter the SMS verification code sent to that number 4. After login: all AI tools functional, "Chats" tab shows conversations NOTE ON PHONE NUMBER REQUIREMENT: Tony Chat uses Telegram's open messaging protocol for authentication and chat. Phone verification is standard for all Telegram-protocol apps (similar to how email clients require email credentials). We do not offer demo accounts because Telegram's protocol requires real phone verification — this is a platform-level requirement, not our design choice. UNIQUE FEATURES (not available in Telegram): • AI Writer — grammar fix, tone adjustment, rewriting • AI Translator — auto-detect and translate any language • Remove Background — transparent PNGs from photos • AI Upscale — enhance image resolution up to 4x • Remove Text — erase text from images • AI Generate — create images from text prompts • AI News Feed — curated articles with AI summaries
iOS-specific: If reviewers have trouble with SMS verification, they can still fully evaluate the app's primary AI features by browsing the AI Hub and Explore tabs without login. The core value proposition (AI tools + news) is accessible without authentication.
