Getting Started

Tony Chat is an AI-powered assistant and messenger. You can browse AI tools and news without logging in. To use AI tools and chat, connect your Telegram account.

How to Get Started

  1. Download Tony Chat from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Browse the AI tools and Explore tab freely (no account needed)
  3. To use AI tools or chat, tap the Chat tab and log in with your Telegram account
  4. Start using AI Writer, Translator, image tools, or chat with friends

AI Tools Guide

AI Writer

Fix grammar, adjust tone, or rewrite text. Paste or type your text, choose an action, and get results instantly. Supports multiple languages.

AI Translator

Auto-detects the source language and translates to your chosen target language. Supports 100+ languages.

Remove Background

Upload a photo and get a transparent PNG with the background removed. Works best with clear subjects (people, products, objects).

AI Upscale

Enhance photo resolution up to 4x. Upload a low-resolution image and get a sharper, higher-quality version.

Remove Text

Automatically detect and remove text from images. Useful for cleaning watermarks or overlaid text from photos.

AI Generate

Create images from text descriptions. Type what you want to see and the AI generates an image for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Tony Chat free to use? +
Yes, Tony Chat is completely free. All AI tools, the news feed, and messaging features are available at no cost. Usage limits may apply to ensure fair access for all users.
Do I need an account to use AI tools? +
You can browse AI tools without an account. To actually use the AI tools (submit text or images for processing), you need to log in with your Telegram account.
Is my data safe? +
Yes. AI processing is stateless — we don't store your text or images after processing. Chat uses the Telegram protocol with end-to-end encryption support. No API keys are stored on your device. See our Privacy Policy and Data Safety Policy for details.
Why do I need a Telegram account? +
Tony Chat uses the Telegram messaging protocol for secure, reliable chat. Your Telegram account provides authentication and access to your existing contacts and chat history. You must be at least 13 years old.
Who owns the content I create with AI tools? +
Content you create using Tony Chat's AI tools belongs to you, subject to the terms of the underlying AI providers. We make no claim of ownership over your AI-generated content. See our EULA for details.
The AI tool isn't working. What should I do? +
  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure you're logged in with your Telegram account
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • If the issue persists, the AI service may be temporarily unavailable — try again in a few minutes
  • For persistent issues, contact us at support@tonychat.app
How do I delete my account or data? +
Tony Chat doesn't maintain a separate account — authentication is through Telegram. To stop using Tony Chat, simply uninstall the app. To request deletion of any data we may hold, email support@tonychat.app. Your Telegram account and chat history are managed by Telegram directly.
What languages does Tony Chat support? +
The AI Translator supports 100+ languages. The AI Writer works best with English, Korean, Japanese, Vietnamese, and other major languages. The app interface is available in English.

Report a Bug

Found a bug or experiencing an issue? Please include the following when contacting us:

  • Your device model and OS version (e.g., iPhone 15, iOS 18)
  • Tony Chat app version (found in Settings)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if possible)
