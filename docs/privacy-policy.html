Tony Chat Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Tony Chat ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered assistant and messaging application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

Tony Chat uses the Telegram messaging protocol for chat functionality. When you connect your Telegram account, we access your Telegram profile (name, phone number, profile photo) solely to enable messaging features.

AI Tool Usage

  • Text tools (AI Writer, Translator): Text you submit is sent to third-party AI providers (Google Gemini, OpenAI) for processing. We do not store your input text or AI-generated results after processing is complete.
  • Image tools (Remove BG, Upscale, Remove Text, AI Generate): Images you submit are sent to ClipDrop API for processing. We do not store your images after processing is complete.

News Feed

The Explore tab displays AI-curated news from publicly available sources. We do not track which articles you read.

Device Information

We may collect basic device information (OS version, device model) for app compatibility and crash reporting purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI tool functionality (text transformation, translation, image processing)
  • To enable secure messaging through the Telegram protocol
  • To display curated news content
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

Tony Chat integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Telegram: Messaging protocol and user authentication
  • Google Gemini: AI text processing
  • ClipDrop: AI image processing
  • Supabase: News feed content delivery

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • AI tool inputs and outputs are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers
  • API keys are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
  • Chat messages are handled by the Telegram protocol with end-to-end encryption support
  • News articles are cached in device memory only and cleared when the app is closed

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with the third-party AI providers listed above, solely for the purpose of providing the requested AI functionality.

7. Your Rights

  • You can use AI tools without creating an account (browse-only mode)
  • You can disconnect your Telegram account at any time
  • All AI processing is stateless - no history is maintained
  • You can request deletion of any data by contacting us

8. Children's Privacy

Tony Chat is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
